## Description (4,000 character max)

PositionPilot is a personal math tool for stock investors who want to understand exactly what happens to their average cost when they buy more shares.

Model a buy before you make it. See how different amounts change your average cost. Compare scenarios side by side. Track your progress over time.

No advice. No predictions. Just clear math on your positions.

---

**Model your buys**
Enter any investment amount and see exactly how it affects your average cost per share — before you commit a dollar.

**Compare scenarios**
Run multiple what-if calculations on the same position. Save the ones worth revisiting. Compare them side by side to see which approach fits your plan.

**Track all your positions**
Add your holdings, keep your average costs up to date, and see your full portfolio at a glance. Works for any stock or asset on any exchange.

**Plan tab**
Each position has a dedicated workspace. The Plan tab shows illustrative buy scenarios at different amounts — a quick way to see what $250, $500, or $1,000 would do to your average cost right now.

**Apply when you're ready**
When you decide to buy, apply the transaction directly in the app. Your average cost and cost basis update immediately. Every buy is logged so you can see your history.

**Progress over time**
See how your average costs have changed since you started tracking. Simple, reflective, and entirely based on your own data.

---

PositionPilot does not provide investment advice, recommendations, or signals of any kind. All calculations are illustrative and based on data you enter. This app is a personal math and scenario modeling tool only.
